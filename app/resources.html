Travel resources

Hotels

Booking.com is a good option for all destinations. For Europe it is the your best choice to find great deals.

To find accommodation in North and South of America, I recommend Expedia.

For hotels in Asia, I recommend using Agoda to find a place to stay.

AirBnb – If you want to stay in a local apartment, here is your 30€ discount for your first booking on AirBnb!

Tours

GetYourGuide is my tour operator of choice. It features a ton of cool activites you can do almost anywhere in the world.

TripAdvisor is a great resource to find tours in specific destinations. It’s also extremly useful when searching for the perfect restaurant.

Flights

Kiwi.com has the most brilliant results when it comes to finding cheap flights. I use it a lot and I recommend it.

lastminute.com is a good resources for finding package deals, including flights.

Travel Guides

My destination page will take you to all the places I wrote about. I want to show you the best ways to spend your time and money.

Lonely Planet is the undisputed leader of paper and digital travel guides. Their guides are made to be of help for any type of vacation and for each budget.

Trains

If you are travelling to Europe you will have a few choices:

Multiple days open tickets (read my cool article about this):

If you want to just buy tickets between European destinations use voyages-sncf.com.

Car Rental

You can find some interesting deals over at Sixt. They have really big fleets to match any taste and need.
